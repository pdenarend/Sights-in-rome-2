]]> In the middle of the Forum Romanum you can still see one pillar standing. This is the Pillar of Phocas. It was erected in honor of Emperor Phocas, who gave the pantheon the Pope, who made it into a church, which is the reason it is still standing. The Pillar was taken from an older building, maybe the round Temple of Vesta which stands next to the Tiber, because that one misses a pillar.
