]]> The piazza in front of the Pantheon is called Piazza della Rotonda. In the middle you can see a fountain, which was made by Giacomo della Porta in 1575. The obelisk that adorns it is from the 13th century BC, from the time of Ramses II. It was stolen by the Romans from Egypt. Romans really liked obelisks and stole so many from Egypt that there are now more Egyptian obelisks in Rome than there are in Egypt.
