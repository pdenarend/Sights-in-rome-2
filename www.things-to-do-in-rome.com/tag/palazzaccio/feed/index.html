]]> To the east of the Castel Sant’Angelo stands the Palace of Justice. It is also called the Palazzaccio. This is roman slang for ugly palace. It was built between 1889 and 1910 by Guglielmo Caldernini. His creation never managed to convince the Romans who have always found this imposing building very ugly. Piazza Cavour, the square that faces the Palazzaccio is a nice place to sit down for a while.
