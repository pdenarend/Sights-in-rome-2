]]> Next to the Sant’Agnese church is Palazzo Pamphili, which now is the Brazilian embassy, but used to be the home of the powerful Pamphili family, the family of Pope Innocentius X. Nowadays this Pope is most famous for the portrait the Spanish artist Diego Velazquez did of him. This painting can be found in the museum Galleria Doria Pamphili, on Via del Corso. Francis Bacon was inspired by this painting for his series of paintings of Screaming Popes.
