]]> Further down on Piazza di Spagna you can see an enormous column. It was found in 1777 close to here and put up in 1856 to commemorate the proclamation of the dogma of the Immaculate Conception. This is why there is a statue of Saint Mary on top. Close to is you see the Spanish flag hanging from a building. This is the Spanish embassy to the Vatican and the reason the Piazza is called Piazza di Spagna.
