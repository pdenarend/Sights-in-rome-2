]]> The Sant’Agnese is a beautiful church on Piazza Navona. Bernini’s rival Borromini worked on it. If you have the chance, have quick look inside because the architecture is beautiful. In the chapel to the left of the choir the head of Saint Agnes is kept. It is called the Sacra Testa. Above the entrance on the inside is the grave of Pope Innocent X.
