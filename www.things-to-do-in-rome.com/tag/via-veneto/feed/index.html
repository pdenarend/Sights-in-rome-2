]]> Via Veneto used to be the most glamorous street in Rome. It was the center of the Dolce Vita in the 60s and 70s. Still, a lot of the most expensive and famous hotels and bars are located on this street. It was made famous by filmmaker Federico Fellini, who shot parts of ‘La dolce vita’ here. Thanks to the success of the movie, the street became soon filled with tourists, who made the international movie stars and Roman elites leave.

The American embassy is also located in an enormous palazzo on this street. Queen Margarita, of pizza fame, used to live here.
