]]> Also in the Via Condotti you can find one of the oldest and most famous coffee bars in Italy, the Antico Caffè Greco. They serve very good coffee in a classic atmosphere. If you drink you espresso standing at the bar it is not expensive at all. The bar was started in 1744. I always go get an espresso here when I’m around here.
