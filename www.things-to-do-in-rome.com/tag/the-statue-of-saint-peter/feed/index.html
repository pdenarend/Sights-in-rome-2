]]> In Saint Peter’s there is a famous statue of Saint Peter. Everybody comes here to touch the right foot of Saint Peter. If you do this you will be blessed. This statue came from the old Saint Peter’s and was made by Arnolfo di Cambio around 1300. You can see Peter’s foot has almost disappeared from all the touching. Every year, on the day of Saint Peter and Paul, the statue is dressed up in Papal robes.
