]]> On Largo Torre Argentina you can see a theatre. It is the most important theatre of Rome. Rossini premiered his Barbiere di Seviglia here. It was a complete disaster. Fans of another composer came to heckle Rossini, who at one point turned around and applauded the spectators. He then had to flee the theatre. The next day the show went according to plan and the Barbiere di Sevigla has been a classic ever since.
