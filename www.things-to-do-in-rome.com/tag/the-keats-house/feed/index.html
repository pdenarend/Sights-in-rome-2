]]> On the right side of the steps you can see the house where the English poet John Keats died in 1821. It now is a small museum dedicated to the poet. He is buried in Rome in the protestant graveyard outside the city walls, which is a nice little excursion if you like to see something different when in Rome. Make sure to check the actual opening times though, the graveyard is often closed.
