Palazzo di Propaganda Fide

Palazzo di Propaganda Fide

April 8, 2015April 8, 2015 Paul den Arend Leave a comment

Behind the column on Piazza di Spagna you can see the flag of the Vatican hanging from a building. This is the Palazzo di Propaganda Fide. It is part of the Vatican and houses the headquarters of the missionaries.
